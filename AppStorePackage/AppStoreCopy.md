## Description
IDPhoto Pro helps you create official-size passport, visa, immigration, and ID photos directly on your iPhone.

Choose a country or document preset, import or take a clear front-facing photo, adjust crop and position, replace the background, run on-device compliance checks, and export a 300 DPI digital or print-ready file.

Key features:
- Official-size templates for passports, visas, immigration cards, and common ID photo formats
- GCC presets for Saudi Iqama, UAE Emirates ID, Qatar residence permit, Kuwait Civil ID, Oman residence card, and Bahrain CPR-style workflows
- On-device compliance checks for face position, head size, sharpness, background, and file size guidance
- Precise crop, zoom, rotation, brightness, contrast, sharpness, and light repair controls
- Plain background replacement with common white, off-white, light gray, and blue options
- JPG, PNG, HEIF, and PDF export
- Target-KB compression for upload portals with strict file-size limits
- 4x6 inch, A4, and Letter print sheet layouts with optional crop marks
- Local creation records, stored only on your device
- One-time purchase, no subscription, no ads

Privacy first:
All photo processing happens on your device. Photos are not uploaded to a server by IDPhoto Pro. The app does not include ad SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs in this version.

Important disclaimer:
IDPhoto Pro is not affiliated with any government, embassy, visa center, or official application portal. The app helps prepare photos based on published requirements, but it cannot guarantee acceptance. Always review the official source before submitting your final photo.
